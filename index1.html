Project Overview

The Recycle Art Tour is an environmental awareness initiative that creatively combines art and sustainability to promote responsible waste management.

This innovative tour uses art as a powerful tool to communicate the dangers of pollution, inspire behavioral change, and empower young people with creative recycling skills.

Mission

To foster environmental consciousness through creative expression, transforming waste materials into artistic treasures while building sustainable communities through youth empowerment.
